About Me

I am a first generation natural born American citizen. I also had the opportunity to spend 7 years in Lebanon, my home county. I have always been into technology since early on in my life. Coupled with my natural curiosity to figure out how and why things work this lead to a lot of mischief, and a lot of learning.

Back in LA after years abroad, I graduated high school and started college while attempting to shoulder the bills. After 5 years toiling with college with semesters on and off to generate the money needed, I came to the conclusion college is no longer worth the time, effort, focus, or costs.

I then used the same work ethic and planning to complete the Lambda School Full-Stack Web Development program. There I used the amazing curriculum structure they have implemented to develop and refine my codeslinging prowess.
